# 示例提示词

以下是几个典型主题的完整提示词输出示例，可直接复制到 GPT Image 2、Nano Banana Pro 等生图工具。

---

## 示例一：五一假日结束

**主题判断**
- 类型：Abstract Concept / Emotion（假期结束，回程）
- 视觉场景：高铁车厢，行李箱，窗外模糊城市
- 推荐标题：五一假日结束
- Layout：C（叠压海报字）
- 色彩：Cool Documentary 冷调纪实

**提示词**

```
Create a premium cinematic Chinese typography poster card. Subject: end of May Day holiday, returning to the city.

SCENE:
Inside a high-speed train, last day of May holiday. Passenger window view — a wheeled suitcase standing upright beside the seat. Outside the window: blurred city skyline approaching, overcast gray sky, motion blur on landscape. Seat fabric in dark gray. A folded paper or ticket stub on the seat. The mood is quiet, slightly melancholic, the holiday is ending. No human faces.

PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE:
Realistic documentary photography quality. Cinematic film still. Cool muted tones: dark charcoal interior, cool blue-gray overcast sky outside. Subtle film grain. Shallow depth of field. NOT illustration. NOT 3D. NOT anime.

TYPOGRAPHY — FOUR ZONES, ALL MUST APPEAR IN FINAL IMAGE:

ZONE 1 — top-left, small:
Two lines of fine-weight Chinese serif text:
"短暂的出发" / "是为了更好地回归生活"
Very small font size, wide letter spacing, left-aligned.

ZONE 2 — top-right, small, right-aligned:
"2026" / "MAY DAY HOLIDAY"
Clean, minimal, like a magazine dateline.

ZONE 3 — LEFT HALF, DOMINANT, LARGE:
Vertical stacked Chinese title: 「五一」「假日」「结束。」
「结」in deep rust red, all others in off-white cream.
Between「假日」and「结束」: italic English line "THE HOLIDAY IS OVER" — fine weight, wide tracking.
Title partially overlaps seat and window edge.

ZONE 4 — BOTTOM:
Bottom-left: "NEXT STOP" / "生活 / 工作 / 现实"
Bottom-right: "VOL.05" / "假期结束特辑" / "——" / "2026-05-05"

COLOR:
Cool charcoal dark interior. Muted blue-gray window sky. Deep rust red accent on「结」only. Off-white title text. NO warm yellow.

NEGATIVE PROMPT:
no garbled Chinese, no missing text zones, no commercial font, no fake logo, no PPT style, no illustration, no anime, no 3D render, no warm filter.

Aspect ratio: 2:3
```

## 示例二：生日快乐（五月六日）

**主题判断**
- 类型：Event / Emotion（生日，34岁）
- 视觉场景：窗边木桌，草莓蛋糕，玫瑰花
- 推荐标题：祝我生日快乐
- Layout：A（大字压图）
- 色彩：Warm Film 暖胶片

**提示词**

```
Create a warm bright cinematic birthday poster for May 6th, 34th birthday.

SCENE:
A wooden table by a large window with bright natural daylight. One small square birthday cake slice with a single lit candle and a strawberry on top, on a white ceramic plate. A glass vase with soft peach roses. An open journal on the table. Bright warm morning light filling the scene — bright, airy, joyful. No party decorations, no balloons.

PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE:
Realistic film photography. Bright warm tones, creamy whites, soft golden light. Subtle film grain. Shallow depth of field. NOT illustration, NOT 3D.

TYPOGRAPHY — layered text:

TOP-LEFT, large dominant vertical title:
「祝我」「生日快乐」
Enormous bold Chinese sans-serif, cream/off-white. Cursive English script overlapping: "Happy Birthday" in warm rust/copper italic.

TOP-CENTER: "05 / 06" / "May. Sixth" — warm copper accent.

TOP-RIGHT small: "又长大了一岁" / "希望这一岁" / "温柔、自由、坚定、闪闪发光"

RIGHT-MIDDLE: "TODAY IS MY DAY." / "05 / 06"

LEFT-MIDDLE paragraph:
"谢谢自己 / 一直在努力生活 / 认真热爱世界 / 也认真对待自己"
— 
"新的一岁 / 继续做喜欢的事 / 成为更好的自己" ♡

BOTTOM-LEFT: "TO LOKI" / "HAPPY BIRTHDAY" / "——" / "2026 / 05 / 06"
BOTTOM-RIGHT: "VOL.34" / "生日限定" / "——"

COLOR:
Bright warm ivory and cream. Soft golden natural light. Warm rust/copper accent. Light, airy — NOT dark, NOT moody.

NEGATIVE PROMPT:
no dark moody tones, no garbled Chinese, no unreadable text, no commercial font, no fake logo, no PPT style, no illustration, no anime, no 3D.

Aspect ratio: 2:3
```
